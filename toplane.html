Illaoi

Be careful when ganking Illaoi after level 6. If she can land her Test of Spirit and Leap of Faith properly, the fight will quickly turn against you.

Teemo

Insane Poke Damage: As we mentioned above, Teemo's poke damage is absolutely through the roof, and he gains a lot from these runes that other champions simply don't get. If the runes are balanced or a little strong for other champions (see Summon Aery), anyone who gets EXTRA benefit from them is going to be over the top.


Maokai

Just be careful that he has bad matchups that require some understanding in both lanes. If you understand the matchup though, you should be able to come out ahead, especially in top lane.
