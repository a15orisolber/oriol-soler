Xin Zhao

Xin Zhao as a champion is quite immobile, even though he has a skill in order to engage [E] and potentialy one to disengage [R] (in most cases just to save his team and not himself), he has no spells in order to escape, because of such nature, Xin Zhao even though he is considered a "low skill" champion by many, in reality he needs a great deal of decision making and most of all positioning and timing of the engage.


Shyvana

You need to earn XP quickly in the first 10 minutes of the game. Shyvana can easily become 2 levels ahead of the enemy jungler, and she scales hard with levels and is hard to beat when she's ahead. You do this by focusing on farming early, using the most efficient routes, and you farm all game, and also gather any XP which is free in lanes (for example while your laner backs).


Rammus

Aftershock is the standard tank keystone right now, and Rammus is no exception from this. It's just a large amount of stats that Rammus makes good use of, followed by a slow that helps him stick to people after the taunt.
