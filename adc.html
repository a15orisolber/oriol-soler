Ezreal

Ezreal is actually the ADC that initially inspired me to start maining ADC , although it was so long ago that I'd almost forgotten that myself. His kit is very fun, and feels extremely rewarding to master given his high skill ceiling.


Miss Fortune

Miss Fortune is a ranged Marksman, AKA AD Carry, a role that focuses on dealing large amounts of physical damage, usually to individual targets. Typically Marksmen scale extremely well with game time, and deal devastating levels of damage during the late phase of games.

In addition to having a very good late game, Miss Fortune excels in lane. She's a lane bully who can dish out tons of burst with her ultimate and can poke extremely efficiently with her Double Up (Q).
